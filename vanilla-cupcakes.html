Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients (Per Person)

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 60g
Sugar 30g
Butter 30g
Egg 0.5
Milk 25ml
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp

Watch How to Make Vanilla Cupcakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream butter and sugar together.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix in the flour and milk alternately until smooth.
  5. Pour batter into cupcake liners.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
